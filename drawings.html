Drawings and sketches

Here you can find all my drawings, sketches and stuff I've made while I was feeling creative. This is a journey through my artistic creations collected over the years. Here you will find a collection of works born spontaneously, guided by the of the moment and the emotions I felt at the moment of creation.

Keep in mind that I have never taken formal lessons or specific drawing courses; everything you will see is the result of a self-taught and intuitive process. I like to explore freely, experiment with different techniques and let myself be carried away by instinct. Each piece is an open window onto my inner world, a fragment of my artistic journey that continues to evolve with me.

I hope you can feel the passion and authenticity I infuse into every stroke and color. Happy exploring!
