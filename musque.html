Project details

Musque

Description

Masque is a luxury fragrance brand renowned for its artisanal perfumes that evoke emotion and elegance. They also retail exclusive collections from other refined brands, including Milano Fragranze, celebrated for its sophisticated and evocative scent profiles.

My role in the project

I was responsible for developing retail graphics to promote Masque’s fragrance lines. This involved creating visually engaging materials that captured the essence of each scent, ensuring a cohesive and luxurious presentation across various in-store touchpoints.

Additional work

I also contributed to designing the teaser and unveiling graphics for Milano Fragranze’s Neon Lights fragrance. My work included creating captivating visual assets that aligned with the product’s identity and amplified anticipation and excitement among customers.
