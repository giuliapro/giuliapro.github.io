Project details

Miryce

Description

Miryce offers a revolutionary line of organic hair care products made with 100% Italian hyperfermented rice water and patented RNP-104 technology. Designed to nourish, protect, and transform hair, Miryce blends the best of nature and innovation to deliver high-performance formulas that are both effective and sustainable.

My role in the project

I was responsible for optimizing the Shopify store by customizing the template and managing product updates from the back-end to ensure the store remained current and fully functional. On the front-end, I reviewed and refined the information and graphics, recreating content to better reflect the brand’s identity. This included designing visuals and writing copy that aligned with Miryce’s values and aesthetic, resulting in a cohesive and engaging online experience that strengthened the brand’s digital presence.

Additional work

I also contributed to managing the brand’s social media presence on Instagram and Facebook. I developed a social media plan to enhance website traffic and highlight the unique qualities of the products. To create impactful posts and stories, I used both design tools and video editing software, and I also leveraged artificial intelligence to generate engaging content aligned with the brand’s tone and objectives.
